Data Sources & Methodology

Every price on FURROW traces to a published source. Here is exactly where the numbers come from and how they are handled.

Staple food crops
Regional wholesale tracking bulletins covering maize, rice, beans, sorghum, millet and potatoes across Tanzania's regions.
Regulated cash crops
Published auction clearing and farm-gate reference rates for coffee, cashew and cotton.
Exchange-traded lots
Tanzania Mercantile Exchange (TMX) electronic auctions, sesame, green gram, soybeans and cocoa warehouse-receipt trading ranges.
How we update
Prices are refreshed with every published bulletin, cross-checked by the FURROW team, and timestamped. Historical snapshots are preserved so trends are never rewritten. Missing regional readings are shown as absent, never estimated.

All prices are indicative reference rates in TZS/kg unless stated otherwise. FURROW publishes this data openly and does not adjust, model or interpolate reported prices.
