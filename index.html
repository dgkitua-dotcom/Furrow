Agri & Transport News
How we source the news

FURROW monitors agricultural and transport news daily across a range of public sources. Each story is summarised in plain language and carries its named source.

We filter for relevance: only developments that affect Tanzania’s agricultural and transport value chain make the board: regulations, levies, market access, inputs and finance. No filler.

Procurement Opportunities
How we vet tenders

Tenders are collected from verified public procurement portals across Tanzania and the region, with the reference number, issuing body, deadline and direct source link for every entry.

Only active opportunities relevant to the agricultural value chain are listed, and expired deadlines are removed with every update, so what you see is always live and worth your time.

The FURROW Platform

What FURROW will do for the market

FURROW is more than a price board. It is a full agricultural trade operating system being built for Tanzania — with four dedicated portals and end-to-end deal management.

How it works
1
Register your role
Sign up as a Supplier, Transporter, or Customer. Your FURROW User ID is assigned instantly. Tanzanian and international participants welcome.
2
Browse verified listings
See live national average prices alongside every supplier’s actual asking price. Filter by crop, region, and grade.
3
Chat and negotiate
Talk directly to suppliers in any of 12 languages. Share sample photos, invoices and certificates in-conversation.
4
Trade with confidence
Place orders and track them through five stages, or ask FURROW to act as your agent, verify the stock and take full liability.
