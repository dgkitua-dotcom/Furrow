Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

FURROW ("we", "us") is operated by Gabrison Capital, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. This policy explains what information we collect on joinfurrow.com and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Email address, if you sign up for platform launch updates, we store the email address you provide. We use it only to notify you about FURROW platform availability and material product updates.
  • Usage analytics, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (pages viewed, approximate location, device type). This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you. See Google's privacy policy for how Google processes this data.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell, rent or trade your email address or any personal information to third parties.
  • We do not send marketing on behalf of advertisers appearing on this site.
  • We do not collect payment information on this website.

Data retention and removal

Launch-update emails are retained until the platform launches or until you ask us to remove yours. To have your email removed at any time, write to info@gabrisoncapital.com and we will delete it promptly.

Third-party links and advertisements

This site displays advertisements for, and links to, third-party businesses. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties. Review their policies before providing them any information.

Changes to this policy

When the FURROW trading platform launches, an expanded privacy policy will govern platform accounts, transactions and mediation services. We will update this page and the "last updated" date when changes are made.
